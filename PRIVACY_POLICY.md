# Privacy Policy for Playback Pilot

Playback Pilot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All functionality occurs entirely on your device. The extension only adjusts the
HTML video playback speed on supported websites. No information about your
browsing activity, identity, or device is accessed or recorded.
